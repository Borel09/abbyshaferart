About the Artist


Abby Shafer is an emerging Louisisiana artist who specializes in architectural photography and digital art. Working from her home studio, she enjoys experimenting with oil paints, pastels, and charcoal. She is always looking for new ways to grow as an Artist.



Abby is currently accepting commistions and hopes to one day open a gallery where a portion of the proceeds will support children with special needs. Her goal is to create art that gives back to the community.
